Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Commitment to Privacy

The Powerball Tax Calculator is designed with privacy as a fundamental principle. We believe your financial calculations should remain completely private and secure.

üîí Key Privacy Features

  • No Data Storage: All calculations are performed locally in your browser
  • No Account Registration: Use our calculator without creating accounts
  • No Personal Information Collection: We don't ask for or store personal details
  • No Tracking: We don't track your usage patterns or financial calculations

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be completely clear about what information we do NOT collect:

  • Financial Information: Your jackpot amounts, tax calculations, or results
  • Personal Identifiers: Names, addresses, phone numbers, or email addresses
  • Government IDs: Social Security numbers, tax ID numbers, or similar identifiers
  • Location Data: Your specific location beyond general geographic region for legal compliance
  • Device Information: Detailed device fingerprinting or unique identifiers
  • Usage History: Your calculation history or previous visits

Technical Information

Like most websites, we may automatically collect basic technical information necessary for website operation:

  • Anonymized Usage Statistics: General page views for website improvement

Important: This technical information cannot be used to identify you personally and is not linked to your calculations.

How Calculations Work

Your privacy is protected by our technical architecture:

  • Client-Side Processing: All calculations happen in your browser
  • No Server Transmission: Your calculation inputs never leave your device
  • Static Tax Data: Tax rates and brackets are pre-loaded, not personalized
  • No Database Storage: No calculation results are saved anywhere

Cookies and Local Storage

Our use of browser storage is minimal and privacy-focused:

  • No Tracking Cookies: We don't use cookies for user tracking
  • Essential Cookies Only: Only technical cookies necessary for website function
  • No Third-Party Cookies: We don't share cookie data with external services
  • Local Storage: May temporarily store user interface preferences (like form state)

You can clear all stored data by clearing your browser cache and cookies.

Third-Party Services

We minimize third-party integrations to protect your privacy:

  • No Analytics Tracking: We don't use Google Analytics or similar services
  • No Social Media Plugins: No Facebook, Twitter, or other social tracking
  • No Advertising Networks: No third-party ads or tracking pixels

We implement multiple layers of security:
